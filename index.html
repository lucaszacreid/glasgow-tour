Your Guide

Meet Ewen

Ewen is a born-and-bred Glaswegian with a deep love for his city's history, architecture, and culture. He has spent years curating the perfect route through Glasgow's most iconic and overlooked landmarks, and his vintage Mercedes is as much a part of the experience as the city itself.

Whether you're visiting Glasgow for the first time or rediscovering a city you thought you knew, Ewen will make sure you leave with stories worth telling. His knowledge runs deep — from the grand Victorian ambitions of George Square to the gritty, glorious industrial heritage of the Clyde — and he delivers it all with the warmth and wit that only a true Glaswegian can.
