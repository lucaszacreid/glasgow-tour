Your Guide

Ewan

Ewan is a born-and-bred Glaswegian with a deep love for his city's history, architecture and culture. He's spent years curating the perfect route through Glasgow's most iconic — and most overlooked — landmarks.

His vintage Mercedes is as much a part of the experience as the city itself. Whether it's your first visit or your fiftieth, Ewan promises stories worth telling and a side of Glasgow you won't find in any guidebook.
